Privacy Policy

How we handle and protect your data.

Last Updated: April 2026

At Sunday Tech Squad Pune, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide to us through our website.

1. Information We Collect

When you book a service through our website, we collect the following personal information:

  • Name: To identify you during our service visit.
  • WhatsApp/Phone Number: To contact you for appointment confirmation and technical coordination.
  • Address & Landmark: To navigate to your location for on-site support.
  • Geolocation (Lat/Long): Captured via your browser (with permission) to help our engineers find your exact doorstep in Pune.
  • Technical Issue Details: To prepare the necessary tools and hardware before our Sunday visit.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Scheduling and fulfilling your Sunday IT support request.
  • Calling you on Saturday evenings for appointment confirmation.
  • Optimizing our travel route for Sunday visits.
  • Procuring the correct hardware (SSD/RAM) based on your device model.

3. Data Protection & Privacy

We take data security seriously. As professional Desktop Support Engineers, we follow industry-standard practices to ensure your data is secure. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties.

4. Google AdSense & Cookies

This website may use Google AdSense to show advertisements. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on this site. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Firebase (Google Cloud) to store our booking data securely. Their privacy policy governs the storage and protection of the data on their servers.

6. Your Consent

By using our site and submitting a booking request, you consent to our website's privacy policy and the collection of the data mentioned above.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact us via WhatsApp or at our service location in Pune.
